A steady shop by the bus stand

Whether you're catching a bus at Kadamba or just passing through Mundvel, Smoke World keeps its shutters up long after most shops close. Locals and travellers alike stop by for a quick restock without a detour.

Floor to ceiling stock — cigarette packs and rolling tobacco, disposable vapes and pod systems, hookah setups, chocolates and munchies, plus the small accessories that are easy to run out of: papers, filters, lighters and coals.

No frills, fair prices, and someone behind the counter who knows the stock. That's the whole idea.
